Firmware Installation
=====================

Remove the PDsid from the C64.



Boot Option 1:
--------------

- Hold down the “Boot” button
- Plug in the USB connector 
- Then release the “Boot” button

A new drive called “RP2350” will now appear on your computer. Copy the firmware file with the .uf2 extension to this drive. 
Wait a moment until the “RP2350” drive disappears again. 
The PDsid is now ready to use




Boot Option 2/3: (if the Boot Button 1 Option is not working)
---------------

-  Use a jumper cable or tweezers and short two pins in the image "Boot_Options.png" (you have 2 options, use one of them)
-  Plug in the USB connector
-  Then remove the cable or tweezers

A new drive called “RP2350” will now appear on your computer. Copy the firmware file with the .uf2 extension to this drive. 
Wait a moment until the “RP2350” drive disappears again. 
The PDsid is now ready to use
